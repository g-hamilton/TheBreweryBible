Meet The Team Nathan Hoare CEO

With a degree in International Business and experience working with businesses in London, Auckland and Sydney, Nathan returns to Devon to combine his passion for beer and desire to grow a new company. Nathan hopes to work with breweries, beer venues and bottle shops all over the world to enhance the beer drinking experience for business and consumer.

 Abby Millar CMO

Abby’s career has been spent helping artisan food and drink producers through to national brands tell their unique stories. With experience in artisan spirits and speciality coffee (and a love for beer) Abby’s role as CMO sees her drive The Brewery Bible brand across the globe, helping shape, improve and inspire the beer drinking experience for beer drinkers everywhere.

 Greg Hamilton CTO

After a 10 year career as a senior airline pilot, Greg now focuses on his passion for creating software and delivering exciting user experiences. Whether it's a trendy taproom, fun festival, undiscovered tasting or a simple, refreshing pint, Greg is working hard to help people all over the world to connect with, learn about and enjoy memorable beer based experiences through The Brewery Bible app.
